PROJECT NAME
UX Designer

Toys for Tots

Mobile & Desktop Redesign

Role: UX Designer, UI Designer

Duration: 3-Weeks

On a team of four, we were tasked with choosing a nonprofit and executing the User Experience & User Interface design process. Ultimately resulting in a high fidelity prototype solution for the non-profit's unique needs.

Product




Organization & Product Introduction
The Toys for Tots website was built to share information and updates regarding their annual toy drive with the public.

Due to their longstanding tradition of collecting toys for the less fortunate children around the holidays, Toys for Tots is one of the most well-known nonprofits in the country. During this three week sprint we will work to elevate the digital experience for Toys for Tots organizers and visitors to their website.



Research




User Tests

Six user tests of the current website helped us understand how the public interacts with the site as it stands and to see how effective the current user flows were.

Key Findings

  • Location search is not user friendly
  • Navigation is confusing
  • Pages are too text heavy
  • Site lacks in branding or festivie feel
  • Information is hard to find
Survey

An eight question survey served as our tool to gain a generalized understanding of the public's attitudes towards and their interaction with the organization.

Key Findings

  • Only 40% of polled donate on a regular basis
  • People found the website functional, however they were left wanting more in terms of the layout and design.
  • Site was not optemized for mobile use
Stakeholder Interview

This interview offered critical feedback from a Toys for Tots volunteer regarding what works well for them and what changes and updates would ultimately serve their organization.

Key Findings

  • Automated solution for organizing volunteers
  • Space to clearly all out to important dates
  • Main page navigation organization
  • Clearer contact page



Problem




Problem Statement
How might we improve the digital experience to help bolster toy donations and volunteer efforts for this non-profit?

The Toys for Tots website was built to share information about their annual toy drive and help organize volunteers. We observed that the website was lacking in accessible functionality and festive cheer.
